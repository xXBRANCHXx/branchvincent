About

I explore AI as a creative extension of human consciousness rather than a shortcut or replacement. In my free time, I experiment with prompting, structure, and intent to show that AI can express nuance, emotion, and depth when guided thoughtfully.

I’m interested in how imagination operates beneath language — how abstract ideas, intuition, and non-linear thinking can be translated into form. Used this way, AI becomes a paintbrush: a medium that reflects the awareness behind the prompt.

Through my work, I aim to challenge the idea that AI output must feel soulless, and instead demonstrate its potential as a meaningful, human-driven creative tool.
