About

I’m Branch Vincent.

My work sits upstream of language, where structure is sensed before it is named. I’m interested in how ideas form prior to explanation, and in what is lost—or distorted—when those ideas are translated into words, images, or systems.

I use AI not as a substitute for thinking, but as a tool for controlled compression: a way to translate pre-conceptual structure into visible form while preserving as much relational integrity as possible.

The focus is not persuasion or authority signaling. It is fidelity—to process, to experience, and to the constraints imposed by translation itself.

This page exists to identify this body of work and its author.
